Overview

Purpose

My purpose of creating this website is to display articles of clothing that will be sold therein, contact information, and general information about the creator and their products. The main page will be used to create interest in the products and the two other child elements will allow viewers to learn more about the products and contact the provider to buy said products.

Audience

My audience will be whoever is interested in purchasing dresses, shirts, and other articles of clothing. This may be college students looking for customized products, high school students for prom dresses, or just natural adjustments to articles of clothing.
