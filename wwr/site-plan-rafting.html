Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to produce quality trips and service to ensure life-time experiences for the whole family. Our trained instructors and guides will give your family the safest and funnest river experience to offer. For more advanced rafters, there are Whitewater rafting tours that will provide an invigorating challenge. In all, we are an entertainment business to give customers the biggest bang for their buck while making memories for a lifetime.

Audience

Target customers range from kids (12-17) to adults changing the difficulty based upon the experience of the rafter. With our competitive pricing, people of all stable financial situations can enjoy our services who want to enjoy the great outdoors. They want to gain experiences and memories through the joys of rafting. Other needs that we don't provide kayaking or other forms of water transportation/recreation. Our audience will be able to access our site through any device.
